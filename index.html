About Amiga Listing

Amiga Alliance is a decentralized Listing platform that focuses on connecting both crypto exchanges and blockchain projects, helping both sides finding the most suitable matches. Projects will be able to submit their listing applications directly to multiple exchanges within ONLY ONE submission, and exchanges can receive the applications directly through the alliance.

Apply Now!

Apply for Listing

For Blockchain projects seeking immediate and direct listings with multiple exchanges in crypto-trading, please follow the listed procedures below and ensure that you click the SUBMIT button to successfully transmit your project’s application to the exchanges directly. Upon receipt of your project’s application, the exchanges will conduct a due diligence review and promptly relay the results. If approved, the exchanges will contact you directly and professionally guide you through the entire listing process.

Step 1

Fill Your Project Information

Please ensure you click the SUBMIT button and fill project information form first.

Step 2

Deliver the Listing Application

Once you submit your listing application successfully, the exchanges you selected will receive your application directly, a due diligence review will be proceeded. During this stage, if you have any questions, you can contact the exchanges or Amiga team for any further supports.

Step 3

Notification & Listing

You will be notified of the review results from the exchanges whether your project pass the review or not. The approved projects will be able to discuss and proceed the listing process with the exchanges, further details & documents will be provided by the exchanges.
