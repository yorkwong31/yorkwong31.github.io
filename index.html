Listing with Exchanges

For Blockchain projects seeking immediate listings with the TOP exchanges in crypto-trading, please follow the listed procedures below and ensure that you click the SUBMIT button to successfully transmit your project’s information to us. Upon receipt of your project’s information, we will conduct a due diligence review and promptly relay the results. If approved, Amiga will professionally guide you through the entire listing process for the exchanges you desire.

Step 1

Enter your Project Information

Please ensure you click the SUBMIT button and fill project information form with us first.

Step 2

Project Review

Our team initiates a due diligence review of your project, normally taking no more than 3-5 business days. During this stage, our team may contact you directly for further information related to your submission.

Step 3

Notification Process

You will be notified immediately of the review results. During this stage, approved projects will receive an engagement letter and detailed information on the next phase of the listing process. Projects that do not pass review will receive an explanation letter and the opportunity to reapply at a later date.
